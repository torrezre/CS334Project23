Paletas

Paletas are a type of frozen treat that originated in Mexico. They are similar to popsicles or ice pops, but are typically made with fresh fruit and other natural ingredients, and often have a creamier texture.

Paletas are a popular dessert or snack in many Latin American countries, and can be found in specialty shops or street vendors in the United States as well.
