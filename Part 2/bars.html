Ice Cream bars

An ice cream bar is a frozen dessert that consists of a single serving of ice cream that is coated in a layer of chocolate, caramel, or other sweet coatings. The ice cream bar is usually served on a stick, making it easy to hold and eat.

Ice cream bars can come in a variety of flavors, such as vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, and mint chocolate chip. They can also have different types of coatings, such as hard chocolate shell or a soft caramel layer. Some ice cream bars also have additional toppings or fillings, such as nuts, sprinkles, or fudge.

Ice cream bars are a popular frozen treat, often sold in grocery stores, ice cream trucks, and at fast food restaurants. They are typically eaten during the summer months or as a dessert after a meal.
