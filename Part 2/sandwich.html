Ice Cream Sandwich

An ice cream sandwich is a dessert that consists of a layer of ice cream sandwiched between two cookies, wafers, or other sweet baked goods. The cookies or wafers are typically soft and chewy, and can be flavored with a variety of ingredients such as chocolate chips, oatmeal, or peanut butter.

The ice cream layer is often made from vanilla or chocolate ice cream, but can also be flavored with different ingredients such as fruit or caramel.
